In this project, a Teacher can input Student Roll numbers:. They ask the Student for Their Number in the format 001, 002, 003. The corresponding student names will automatically appear. Then, by clicking an "Add Data" button:, the teacher can continue adding more students.

Once all the students are added, there's a button to generate an Excel sheet with the attendance data. If the Excel file already exists, clicking on the "Generate Excel" button: automatically downloads the existing file, and the attendance data is appended to it.

After the Excel sheet is generated, a link or button appears, allowing the teacher to download the file. Clicking this "Download Excel" button initiates the download process, saving the Excel file to the user's designated download folder on their computer.

In order to streamline attendance management, our tool offers two convenient options for teachers:

Merge Attendance: To compile attendance data from 30 days into a single sheet, simply click on 'Merge Excel Sheets'. The tool will automatically merge all the Excel sheets in ascending order and display the consolidated data on the screen.

Download Full Data: If the teacher prefers to have the complete attendance data in an Excel format, they can click on 'Download as Excel'. This option allows them to download the entire dataset for further analysis or record-keeping.

Additionally, for easy printing or conversion to PDF format, we provide a 'Print' button. By clicking on it, users can seamlessly print out the attendance records or convert them into a PDF document .
